# Your AI OS — by OTIUM

A personal AI Operating System that runs inside VSCode with Claude. It knows who you are, manages your calendar and tasks, and files your thoughts where they belong — so you get your time back.

## The 5 commands

| Say | What happens |
|---|---|
| `onboard` | Set up (or update) your profile — Claude interviews you |
| `calendar` | Put any event on your Google Calendar, conflict-checked |
| `tasks` | Add, check, and complete your to-dos; due items surface automatically |
| `capture` | Throw any thought at Claude — it files it in the right place |
| `doctor` | Something broken? This diagnoses and fixes it step by step |

You don't need to memorize these — just talk normally. Claude picks the right one.
