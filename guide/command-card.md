# Command Card — keep this open in week 1

Talk to Claude like you'd talk to a sharp assistant. These five words are shortcuts, not magic spells:

1. **onboard** — "Claude, onboard" → set up or update your profile
2. **calendar** — "put lunch with Andi on Friday 12:00 on my calendar"
3. **tasks** — "add task: pay supplier before the 28th" · "what's on my plate?"
4. **capture** — "capture this: customer X prefers WhatsApp over email"
5. **doctor** — anything looks broken or red → just type "doctor"

Daily habit that makes this stick: **open VSCode with your morning coffee, ask "what's on my plate today?"** — Claude checks your tasks and calendar for you.
